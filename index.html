Easy peanut butter cookies

Ingredients:
-150g crunchy peanut butter
-75g coconut sugar (you can substitute for normal suggar if you want)
-1 tbsp ground flax seeds + 3 tbsp water
-Dark chocolates for dipping
Method:
1. In a small bowl combine the flax seeds and the water. Set aside for 5 mins.
2. In a bigger bowl add the peanut butter, coconut suggar and flax seed mixture. Mix until well combined.
3. Form little balls with the dough, lay them on a baking sheet and flatten them with a fork.
4. Bake at 180°c for 10 mins. Once they’re ready, let them cool down completely.
5. Finally dip them in melted drak chocolate and sprinkle flaky salt on top! Leave them in the fridge for around 30mins for the chocolate to harden.

Couronne des rois

Ingredients:
-500g flour
-1 packet dry yeast (7g)
-1 tsp sal
-1 packet of vainilla sugar
-50g suggar
-3 dl waem plant milk
-Juice of ¼ a lemon
-100g of vegan butter
-Raisins or chocolate chips (opcional)
-1 almond
-Topping pearl suggar and shaved almonds.
Method:
1. In a bid bowl combine the flour, dry yeast, salt, vanilla suggar and suggar.
2. Add in the warm milk, lemon juice, vegan butter and mix.
3. Once you get a smooth dough, transfer it into a bowl and cover with a kitchen towel. Let it rest in a warm place for an hour.
4. Knead it with your hands again, add raisins and the almond an form 10 indentical sized balls. Combine 2 of them into one and that one will be the middle one. Place the 8 others around it on the baking sheet. Let it rest of another 30 minswith a towel over it.
5. Brush with melted vegan butter and sprinkle pearl suggar and shave almonds ontop. Bake at 190°c for 25mins.
